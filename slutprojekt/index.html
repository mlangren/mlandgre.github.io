Austin Richard Post was born on july 4th 1995 in Syracuse, New york, America, where he lived for 9 years until he moved to Texas.
He grew up with his mother Nicole Frazier Lake and his father Rich Post.
His father had ones been a DJ himself and introduced Post to music, soon after, Post started playing the guitar; little did he know back then that this would be the beginning of his future career.
During his early years he got rejected to a band after a failed audition.
He never gave up on his dreams and when he was 16 he made his first mixtape in FL Studio. It proved potential and many of his friends believed that he would one day become famous.
Later on he appeared on a DJ set playing music made by the emo band My chemical romance and thus made a name for himself.
Post moved to Los Angeles together with several other producers. They formed a group called BLCKVRD and started creating music together.
It was in LA that he met the producers who would help him create his first hit "White iversion". This song got him the attention he deserved. He got praised by several known artist such as Mac Miller and Wiz Khalifa and shortly after signed a record label and started his career for real.
